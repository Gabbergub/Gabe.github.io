TABU
2024
Type Design
Layout Design
Typography
[About]
TABU is a meticulously crafted regular-weight sans serif typeface inspired by my deep appreciation for the aesthetic and storytelling of horror movies. The typeface captures the raw and unsettling qualities of horror films, blending organic textures with a clean, minimalist sans serif form. The "stick-bound" design evokes imagery of forest rituals, survivalist themes, and the fragile balance between nature and human intervention—a recurring element in classic and modern horror alike.

[Project]
Got a Light?
2024
Animation
Motion
[About]
Originally designed as a loading throbber for a website. The concept behind this particular throbber is inspired by the transient nature of a lighter—an everyday object that is often passed from person to person and, more often than not, ends up lost or in someone else’s pocket. This end product is a visual metaphor: just as a webpage loads in an endless loop until it is ready, a lighter circulates within social interactions, sometimes returning to its owner, sometimes disappearing into the unknown.
