Hi there, I'm Gabe! I'm a graphic designer and self-taught artist based in Brooklyn, New York. My work is heavily influenced by my background in drawing and painting.

I have 24 years of experience with pencil and charcoal, which have become my main go-to art mediums. My artistic journey began with cartooning at age six after I was gifted a drawing of the then-popular Pokémon character, Mewtwo. My desire for creative growth led me to explore different mediums and techniques, eventually transitioning seamlessly into portrait drawing by my early teenage years.

Recently, I completed a degree in Communication Design at Parsons School of Design. I continue to nurture and expand my artistic skills, anticipating the fusion of design principles with my passion for art.

I Currently teach Architecture and Design to children ages 5–9 at Kids In Design.
