Hi there, I'm Gabe!

I'm a graphic designer and self-taught artist based in Brooklyn, New York. My work is heavily influenced by my background in drawing and painting.

I have 24 years of experience with pencil and charcoal, which have become my main go-to art mediums. My artistic journey began with cartooning at age six after I was gifted a drawing of the then-popular Pokémon character, Mewtwo. My desire for creative growth led me to explore different mediums and techniques, eventually transitioning seamlessly into portrait drawing by my early teenage years.

Presently, I am studying Communication Design at Parsons School of Design. While pursuing my academic endeavors, I remain committed to nurturing and expanding my artistic repertoire, anticipating the fusion of design principles with my passion for art.

When I am not immersed in my studies, I work as a Teacher at Kids In Design, teaching Architecture and Design to children ages 5-9 years-old.
