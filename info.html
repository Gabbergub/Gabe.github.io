About
Hi, I'm Gabe!

I’m a graphic designer and self-taught artist based in Brooklyn, New York, with a solid experience in drawing. I’ve been working with pencil and charcoal for over 24 years — they’ve really become my go-to mediums. My creative journey started when I was six, after someone gave me a drawing of Mewtwo, the Pokémon character. That moment sparked something in me, and I began drawing right away. Over time, my curiosity grew, and I started exploring new techniques and art mediums. Eventually I found my way into portrait drawing by my early teens.

I’m a recent graduate of the Communication Design program at Parsons School of Design. Since graduating, I’ve continued to practice and expand my artistic and design skills.

I’m currently working as a Creative Intern at the Daily Mail and as a Coordinator & Studio Tech at the 92NY Art Center.
